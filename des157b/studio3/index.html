insights

  • apple music favors generality and parent genres over specifics and subgenres
  • spotify has massive subgenre classification
  • apple music allows library sorting and manual classification by genre while spotify does not
  • spotify de-emphasizes genre despite incredible genre filtering
  • spotify assigns genre by artist rather than song/album
  • apple music assigns genre by song/album rather than genre
  • apple has moderate subgenre classification for alternative and rock music
  • apple has obscenely low subgenre classification for the "Hip-Hop/Rap" umbrella
  • apple has low subgenre classification for the "World" umbrella -- most music outside of the US is simply classified as the name of the respective continent or "World" eg. "African"
  • spotify acknowledges geographic origin and attatches genre to the end -- eg. "Brazilian Jazz" instead of "Brazilian"
  • apple does allow manual metadata editing capabilities -- user can classify all of their songs if they want to
  • spotify genre classification seems more for algorithmic purposes -- when searching for deep subgenres only results are user-created playlist and nothing officially sorted by spotify
