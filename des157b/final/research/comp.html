Music Challenge

The Pudding

https://pudding.cool/2020/04/music-challenge/

This website prompts the user to enter their birth year, then try their best to identify songs from different decades. After 10 songs, they display your results and a write up on how music has been passed to different generations. I'd like to base my website off of this in terms of interaction from the user. I think it would be fun to let the user interact with art on the website itself and contexutalize themselves within the context of the website. instead of just reading data that I have accumulated.

This website charts the "timelessness" of songs through spotify data. In other words, which hits from past decades have maintained high streaming numbers and which have faded into obscurity.
